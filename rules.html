Discord These are the rules for my Discord server. It is important that you follow these rules to make sure that my community stays the way it should. Failure to follow some of these may result in a timeout/mute, tempban, or a perm ban.
  • Do your best to follow the rules set by Discord. This means following the ToS and Guidelines
  • Keep the chats friendly, if you need to debate go over to #debates
  • Don't spoil things. If discussing an event that may include spoilers, use the spoiler feature by enclosing your messages in ||.
  • Don't promote yourself, I plan to make a channel for this soon.
  • Respect other people's opinions. We are human; we don't agree with everything that we talk about.
  • Don't discuss religious beliefs in a forceful way. This means don't push your religion on other people.
  • If someone asks you to stop discussing religious topics, please do so.
  • If someone asks you to stop discussing other sensitive topics, please do so. (I will be changing this to be more specific in the future.)
  • Don't beg for roles, tiers, perms, or other things like this. Tiers aren't given away for free, and perms like sending messages in channels are unlocked by talking and engaging in the server and unlocking levels.
  • Don't beg to join sessions or games with me (or other people). Personally, I'm not comfortable with streaming content with people I'm unfamiliar with. This is due to me being a bit antisocial, but mostly because I see this as a career and I can't risk something happening on stream that may damage my reputation.
  • We don't tolerate bullying or making fun of certain things (such as, but not limited to, sexual orientation). When this rule is broken involving things like sexual orientation, you will most likely get banned. I accept people for who they are, I do not tolerate people making fun of someone's sexual orientation or hobbies. This rule is formed this way to let you know I accept people that are part of the LGBTQ community, people that are furries, therians, etc.
  • If you chargeback on a donation or anything similar you will immediately be banned from all platforms. This is NOT tolerated whatsoever. Charging back not only is a scummy thing to do, but it also takes a chargeback fee from me as well.
